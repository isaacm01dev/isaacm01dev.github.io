Our Restaurant

Certainly, it is an ancient building, with a lot of history and memories, but with an innovative mindset inside.
We hope this blend will make you all enjoy the comfort of a family place together with a dynamic and changing kitchen style and pleasent gastronomic experience.

Our Menu

We believe every culinaire style can be as excellent as simple if it's prepared with the best ingredients and in the way it deserves. We make a selection of several products and styles to achieve the best experience for our costumers who come back to try whatever the author in the kitchen has created for them.

Our Story

It all started back in 1948 when Grandpa Dareios, founded a humble bakery in this same place that after three generations would become our current restaurant.

Those were hard times but since then, we have always been concerned about giving a good customer service and high quality in our products. Over the years, we have been improving our dishes and those who have tried our specialties always come back.
